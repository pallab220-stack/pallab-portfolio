About Me

Story-first wedding films with a premium cinematic finish.

Hi, I am Pallab Debnath, the editor behind Pallab Films. I help wedding filmmakers and creative studios turn raw wedding footage into polished films that feel emotional, elegant, and cinematic.

My editing style is built around timing, music, clean dialogue, luxury color tone, and story flow. From intimate teasers to full wedding highlights, I shape each project so the couple's day feels honest, refined, and unforgettable.
